Hey, I'm Jess

I'm a behavioral neuroscientist.

Currently, I'm a Ph.D. Student in Dr. John Tuthill's lab at the University of Washington, and plan to graduate Fall 2025.

I am a 2018 UC Santa Cruz alum. For two years following my undergraduate career, I worked as a research specialist at the University of Pennsylvania in Dr. Ishmail Abdus-Saboor's Lab. I developed new, more objective assays and computational tools to assess acute and chronic pain states in rodent models.

Now, I hope to develop a strategy to delineate reflexive and affective neuronal pathways downstream of Drosophila nociceptors via behavioral experiments and comprehensive neural circuit reconstruction.
